About Physics Calc

Physics Calc is an open educational platform designed to provide accessible computational tools for undergraduate students, graduate researchers, and educators. Built to bridge the gap between textbook theory and rapid numerical verification, with equations rendered dynamically via KaTeX.


Areas covered:

  • General Physics & Mechanics
  • Optics, Oscillations & Waves
  • Quantum Mechanics & Modern Physics
  • Solid State Physics & Materials Science
  • Scientific Computing & Research Utilities
